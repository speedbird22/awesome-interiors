The Awesome Interiors Foundation

The Awesome Interiors Foundation was established to celebrate the power of design as a force for positive change — and to support the next generation of interior design talent in India.

Founded by Pallavi Reddy, the Foundation provides mentorship, resources, and financial support to aspiring designers from Hyderabad and across Telangana, helping them build the skills and confidence to shape beautiful, meaningful spaces.

Through partnerships with design colleges and community initiatives, the Foundation champions creativity, craftsmanship, and the belief that great design should be accessible to all.

Our Vision

We believe that talent has no postcode. The Foundation exists to ensure that passion and creative potential can be nurtured and realised.
