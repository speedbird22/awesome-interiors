Story

Awesome Interiors was founded in 2014 by Pallavi Reddy with a clear vision — to deliver beautifully crafted interior spaces that reflect the personality and aspirations of each client.

Operating from Hyderabad, Telangana, the studio has built a strong reputation for residential and commercial interior design, combining thoughtful space planning with meticulous material selection and hands-on project management.

Every project is approached with the same philosophy: listen deeply, design authentically, and execute with precision. From cosy home renovations to large-scale commercial fitouts, Awesome Interiors brings creative excellence and reliable delivery to every brief.

Over more than a decade, the studio has earned lasting client relationships built on trust, transparency, and a consistent commitment to quality.

"Great interiors don't just look beautiful — they feel right. Every space we create is designed to be lived in, loved, and remembered."
Pallavi Reddy, Founder

Founder

Pallavi Reddy is the Founder and Owner of Awesome Interiors, based in Hyderabad, Telangana. Since establishing the studio in 2014, she has led every aspect of the business — from client consultation and concept development through to procurement, scheduling, vendor coordination, and final project handover.

With a hands-on approach to design and an unwavering focus on quality, Pallavi has cultivated a studio known for its reliability, creative rigour, and genuine care for client outcomes.

Her expertise spans residential and commercial interiors, and she brings the same level of dedication to every project — whether a private home, a boutique retail space, or a corporate environment.
